## Information Security Policy

3B Credit Builder is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity,
and availability of all information assets.

### Objectives
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Enforce least privilege
- Maintain auditability
- Protect regulated data
- Detect and respond to incidents

### Enforcement
This policy is enforced through:
- Analyzer-driven controls
- Vault lifecycle enforcement
- Role and mapping validation
- Continuous monitoring and logging

Violations are logged and investigated.
