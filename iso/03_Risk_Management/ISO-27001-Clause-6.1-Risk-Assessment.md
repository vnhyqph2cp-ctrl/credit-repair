## Information Security Risk Assessment

### Method
Risks are evaluated based on likelihood and impact.

### Identified Risks
- Unauthorized document access
- Cross-tenant data exposure
- Evidence tampering
- Insider misuse
- Third-party compromise

### Controls
- RLS and mapping enforcement
- Signed URLs
- Verification states
- Audit logs
- Encrypted storage

Risk reviews occur regularly.
